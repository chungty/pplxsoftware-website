Common Questions

  • Install TeamOps from the Atlassian Marketplace. It works in 60 seconds with zero configuration.
  • Go to Apps > TeamOps > Team Directory > Add Employee.
  • Go to Apps > TeamOps > Onboarding > Start Onboarding > pick a template.
  • Go to Apps > TeamOps > Offboarding > select a template > enter the employee's last day.
  • All data stays within Atlassian's infrastructure. TeamOps is Forge-native. PPLX Software has zero access to your data.
  • Your Jira admin can upgrade from the Atlassian Marketplace billing page.
